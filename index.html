Welcome to The Travel Hack!


Hello and welcome to The Travel Hack, a travel blog about weekend breaks and my travel hacks and tips to help you travel more, see more and experience more!

My name is Anna and I’ve been running this blog for 10 years. I started as a budget backpacker and this blog has evolved with me over the years as I’ve grown and had children. The Travel Hack is now one of the leading travel blogs with over 150,000 monthly readers!
